5. ABSOLUTE PROHIBITION ON COMMERCIAL & RESTRICTED ACTIVITIES

STRICTLY PROHIBITED ACTIVITIES

The Tenant shall NOT, under any circumstances, and whether directly or indirectly:

  • Subletting & Unauthorized Occupancy: Sublet, license, share, assign, or part with possession of whole/any part of premises
  • Commercial Activities: Any business, profession, trade, office, storage, warehouse, industrial, commercial, techno-commercial activity (online/offline)
  • Registered Offices: Use premises as registered office or for GST, MSME, LLP/Company registration
  • Data Center Operations: Storage of servers, IT equipment, or data center hosting operations
  • Social Gatherings: Events/parties beyond normal family use causing nuisance/noise
  • Hazardous Storage: Goods, chemicals, inflammables, commercial inventory beyond household items
MATERIAL BREACH CONSEQUENCES: Immediate termination, full security deposit forfeiture, tenant indemnification.[file:1]
