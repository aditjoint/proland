PERMITTED USE & ABSOLUTE PROHIBITIONS

STRICTLY RESIDENTIAL USE ONLY

The Tenant shall use the Demised Premises SOLELY AND EXCLUSIVELY for residential purposes of the Tenant and approved family members ONLY.

ABSOLUTE PROHIBITION on the following activities (Material Breach):

  • Subletting, licensing, sharing, assigning or parting with possession of whole/any part of premises.
  • Any business, profession, trade, office, storage, warehouse, industrial, commercial, techno-commercial or consultancy activity (online/offline).
  • Using premises as registered office, branch office, liaison office or correspondence address for any proprietorship, partnership, LLP, company, trust, society or entity.
  • Obtaining/using address for GST registration, Shops & Establishments, MSME, LLP/Company incorporation, tax registrations or ANY statutory license.
  • Social gatherings, events, parties beyond normal family use causing nuisance, noise or increased footfall.
  • Storage of goods, equipment, chemicals, inflammables, commercial inventory, servers, IT/network equipment for data centre/business operations.
CONSEQUENCES OF VIOLATION: Immediate termination, full security deposit forfeiture, tenant to withdraw all registrations at own cost, full indemnification to Owner.[file:1]

TENANT OBLIGATIONS & RESTRICTIONS

  • Occupancy Limit: Maximum 4 persons (2 adults + 2 children). Strictly enforced.
  • Guests: Must vacate by 11:00 PM. Long-term guests (>15 days) require written approval.
  • No Pets/Smoking: Strictly prohibited without written consent.
  • Utilities: Tenant responsible for electricity, water, gas, internet, maintenance charges.
  • Maintenance: Tenant maintains interior cleaning, minor repairs, AC servicing. Owner maintains structure, external areas.
  • Quiet Hours: No noise/disturbance 10:00 PM - 6:00 AM.

TERMINATION & LEGAL PROVISIONS

  • Notice Period: 60 days written notice from either party.
  • Overstay Penalty: Double rent per day until vacation.
  • Deposit Refund: Within 30 days after deductions (itemized invoice provided).
  • Governing Law: Indian Contract Act, 1872 & Maharashtra tenancy laws.[web:3]
  • Jurisdiction: Courts in Navi Mumbai shall have exclusive jurisdiction.
