OWNER'S RIGHT TO INSPECT

Owner may enter premises with 24-hour written notice for:

  • Maintenance, repairs, or emergency response
  • Pest control or inspections
  • Showing property to prospective tenants (last 60 days of lease)

Emergency Entry (No Notice): Fire, flood, gas leak, or safety hazard.

DEFAULT & EVICTION

Material Breach Grounds (Immediate Eviction):

  • Rent unpaid for 30 consecutive days
  • Commercial activity or GST registration at address
  • Subletting or unauthorized occupants discovered
  • Structural damage or safety hazards created

Overstaying: If Tenant remains after lease expiry, Owner may charge double rent for overstay period and pursue immediate eviction.

DEPOSIT REFUND & FINAL SETTLEMENT

Security deposit refundable within 30 days after lease termination, deducting:

  • Unpaid rent or utilities (with bills)
  • Damage beyond normal wear and tear (with repair receipts)
  • Outstanding society/HOA dues
  • Cleaning charges if property returned dirty
  • Tenant is liable to pay balance expenses if above charges exceed. due to property returned in cosmetically, physically damaged and dirty conditions

Itemized deduction invoice provided to Tenant. Excess refunded via bank transfer within 45 days.
