1. NATURE OF AGREEMENT – LEAVE AND LICENSE ONLY
1.1 No Tenancy or Leasehold Interest

The parties expressly agree and acknowledge that:

  • NO TENANCY, LEASE, SUB-TENANCY, or any right, title, or interest in the Licensed Premises shall be created in favour of the Licensee.
  • The Licensee is a mere licensee with personal, temporary, and revocable permission to use and occupy the Licensed Premises for residential purposes only.
  • The Licensee shall NOT acquire, claim, or be entitled to any protection under:
    • Maharashtra Rent Control Act, 1999 (or applicable state tenancy laws)
    • Model Tenancy Act, 2021
    • Any local rent control or tenancy protection statutes
    • Any statutory succession or inheritance rights
    • Any right of renewal or continuation beyond the stated license period
1.2 Licensor Retains Possession and Control
  • Ultimate possession, control, and ownership of the Licensed Premises remain with the Licensor at all times.
  • The Licensee's right is purely contractual and personal and can be revoked by the Licensor as per the terms of this Agreement.
  • The Licensor may at any time revoke the license and recover vacant possession of the Licensed Premises without the Licensee claiming any tenancy protection or statutory rights.
1.3 Non-Assignment and Non-Transferability
  • The Licensee shall NOT assign, transfer, sublet, share, or part with possession of the Licensed Premises or any part thereof to any person, firm, company, or entity.
  • Any purported transfer or sublet shall be void and constitute material breach of this Agreement, entitling the Licensor to immediate revocation and recovery of possession.
1.4 Governing Law
  • This Agreement is governed by:
    • Indian Easements Act, 1882 (primary statute for leave and license)
