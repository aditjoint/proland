✅ OWNER PROTECTION CLAUSES (AUTOMATIC)

Your agreement will include 30+ protective clauses:

  • NO TENANCY RIGHTS: Absolute protection - occupant has zero tenancy/lease rights under Rent Control Acts
  • FULL LEGAL POSSESSION: Owner retains complete possession, title & control at all times
  • STRICT RESIDENTIAL ONLY: ZERO commercial/business/GST/MSME use permitted - INSTANT EVICTION
  • LIMITED OCCUPANTS: ONLY listed members permitted - additional persons = immediate termination
  • IDENTITY VERIFICATION: All members' names, ages, ID proof declared - NO CHANGE permitted
  • NUISANCE CLAUSE: Any noise, disturbance, antisocial behavior = IMMEDIATE EVICTION
  • CRIMINAL ACTIVITY: ANY criminal complaint/FIR = AUTO-TERMINATION (no notice)
  • LEASE DEFAULT: Non-payment >3 days = immediate eviction proceedings
  • PROPERTY DAMAGE: Full liability for damage beyond normal wear - 3x deposit forfeiture
  • UNAUTHORIZED GUESTS: Overnight guests for >7 days = material breach
  • INSPECTION RIGHTS: Owner unrestricted entry for inspection/repairs with 24hr notice
  • LIEN ON GOODS: Owner has paramount lien on occupant's goods for unpaid dues
  • DEPOSIT FORFEITURE: Full/partial deposit forfeiture for violations without refund
  • LATE PAYMENT PENALTY: 2% monthly penalty on overdue fees + 18% annual interest
  • UTILITIES: All consumption charges (electricity, water, gas) on occupant
  • IMMEDIATE VACATION: Occupant must vacate within 24 hours of termination notice
  • HOLDOVER PENALTY: ₹2,000/day penalty for every day after termination date
  • STATUTORY COMPLIANCE: Owner indemnified for GST/Tax/Municipal violations by occupant
  • DISPUTE RESOLUTION: Arbitration by owner-nominated single arbitrator
  • AUTOMATIC TERMINATION: Auto-termination on nuisance/criminal/commercial misuse
