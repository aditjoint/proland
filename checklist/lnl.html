✓ Included Legal Provisions (50+ Clauses)

This agreement incorporates comprehensive legally binding provisions protecting the property owner:

  • No Tenancy Creation: Arrangement creates License ONLY under Indian Easements Act 1882. Occupant acquires NO tenancy rights, leasehold interest, or statutory protection under any Rent Control Act.
  • Ownership & Possession: Licensor retains absolute legal title, possession, and control. Licensee receives only revocable right of occupation.
  • Residential Use Covenant: Premises exclusively for residential purposes by declared occupants ONLY. NO commercial, business, or income-generating use.
  • Restricted Occupancy: ONLY listed occupants permitted. NO additional persons without prior written consent. Unauthorized occupation = immediate breach.
  • Identity Documentation: All occupants' names, ages, identification proof, and relationships are FIXED. NO substitution or change permitted during term.
  • Guest Policy & Duration: Short-term overnight guests permitted maximum 7 consecutive days. Extended visits require written permission.
  • Nuisance & Disturbance: ANY noise, music, gathering, or activity causing disturbance to neighbors = immediate breach. Licensor may terminate summarily.
  • Criminal Activity Clause: If any occupant subject to criminal complaint, FIR filing, arrest, or police investigation = agreement terminates IMMEDIATELY without notice.
  • Non-Compliance With Society Rules: Violation of society bylaws = breach. Licensor may terminate if occupant receives complaints from authorities or management.
  • Payment Obligations: Monthly license fee due by specified date. Late payment beyond 3 days = 2% monthly penalty PLUS 18% annual interest on overdue amount.
  • Property Damage Liability: Occupant liable for ALL damage beyond normal wear and tear. Willful damage = forfeiture of 3x deposit amount PLUS repair costs.
  • Security Deposit Terms: Deposit held in trust. Refundable subject to NO damage, unpaid fees, or violations. Full or partial forfeiture applies for breaches.
  • Utilities & Consumption: ALL electricity, water, gas, internet, and consumption-based charges = occupant's responsibility. Bills must be paid timely.
  • Inspection & Access Rights: Licensor retains unrestricted right to enter for inspection, repairs, or viewing by prospective licensees, with 24 hours notice (except emergencies).
  • Lien on Occupant's Property: Licensor has paramount lien on ALL goods and chattels within premises for unpaid fees and dues. Items may be seized after 15 days notice.
  • Notice of Termination: Licensor may terminate with immediate effect for material breach. Occupant must vacate within 24 hours of termination notice.
  • Holdover Charges: If occupant remains after termination, penalty of ₹2,000 per day applies for each day of unauthorized occupation.
  • Vacant Handover: Upon termination or expiry, occupant must deliver vacant, clean possession. ALL keys must be returned. Professional cleaning certification may be required.
  • Sub-letting & Transfer Prohibition: Occupant may NOT sub-let, assign, or transfer license. ANY attempt = grounds for immediate termination.
  • Prohibition on Statutory Registration: Occupant confirms NO GST, professional tax, MSME, or commercial registration shall be obtained against premises address.
  • Indemnity for Statutory Violations: Occupant indemnifies licensor against ALL GST demands, tax notices, municipal penalties, or statutory action from occupant's misuse.
  • Insurance & Liability: Occupant maintains responsibility for contents insurance. Licensor NOT liable for theft, loss, or damage to occupant's belongings.
  • Society & Regulatory Compliance: Occupant shall comply with ALL society bylaws, municipal rules, and applicable building regulations. Violations = grounds for termination.
  • Prohibited Activities Auto-Termination: Following constitute immediate grounds for termination WITHOUT notice: (a) commercial activity, (b) criminal conduct, (c) nuisance, (d) unauthorized occupants, (e) property damage, (f) non-payment, (g) statutory violations.
  • Pets & Animals: Pets NOT permitted unless explicitly authorized in writing. Unauthorized animals = breach and immediate eviction.
  • Alterations & Modifications: NO structural changes, alterations, or modifications permitted without written consent. Unauthorized changes = forfeiture of deposit.
  • Default Interest: ALL overdue amounts attract interest at 18% per annum calculated monthly. IN ADDITION to late payment penalty.
  • Dispute Resolution & Arbitration: Disputes shall be resolved through arbitration by single arbitrator appointed by licensor. Arbitration award is BINDING and enforceable.
  • Governing Law & Jurisdiction: Agreement governed by laws of respective state and Indian contract law. Jurisdiction vests EXCLUSIVELY in courts of district where premises located.
  • Waiver of Tenant Rights: Occupant expressly waives ANY claim to tenancy rights, statutory protection, rent control benefits, or succession rights under ANY law.
  • Severability: If any clause found invalid, remaining clauses remain enforceable. Invalid clause shall be replaced by closest valid alternative.
  • Amendment & Modification: NO modification valid unless in writing and signed by both parties. Verbal modifications NOT binding.
  • Notice Provisions: ALL notices shall be delivered personally, by email, or registered post to addresses mentioned. Notice effective upon receipt.
  • Renewal & Extension: Upon expiry, agreement terminates automatically unless renewed in writing BEFORE expiration date. NO automatic renewal.
  • Financial Responsibility: Occupant shall bear ALL expenses including: (a) utilities, (b) society charges, (c) property maintenance contribution, (d) insurance.
  • Medical & Injury Liability: Licensor NOT liable for any injury, illness, or accident sustained by occupant within premises. Occupant assumes FULL risk.
  • Privacy & Surveillance: Installation of CCTV or recording devices in common areas permitted. Occupant has NO privacy rights in common areas.
  • Communication Clause: Licensor may communicate notices via email, SMS, or post. Occupant acknowledges these are valid methods of notice.
  • Force Majeure: Neither party liable for non-performance due to natural disasters, government action, or circumstances beyond reasonable control.
  • Right to Repossession: Upon termination, licensor may repossess premises and remove occupant's belongings if not vacated timely. Removal costs = occupant's liability.
  • Escalation of Fees: Licensor may increase license fee on annual renewal by up to 10% with 60 days written notice.
  • Legal Costs Recovery: In case of dispute, losing party shall bear ALL legal costs, advocate fees, and court expenses incurred by winning party.
  • Records & Documentation: Occupant shall maintain records of payment and produce upon demand. Failure to produce records = treated as non-payment.
  • Maintenance Charges: Any maintenance charges are SEPARATE from license fee and are non-refundable.
  • Government Notification Compliance: Both parties shall comply with all government notifications, guidelines, and circulars issued from time to time.
  • Lockdown & Pandemic Clauses: During government-imposed lockdown or force majeure events, license fee shall REMAIN DUE.
  • Default Remedies: All remedies cumulative. Licensor's acceptance of late fees/deposits NOT waiver of any rights.
  • Occupant's Representations: Occupant represents being of sound mind, NOT under any legal disability, and having authority to execute this agreement.
  • Binding Effect: This agreement shall be binding on heirs, executors, administrators, and permitted assigns of both parties.
  • Entire Agreement: This constitutes the entire agreement between parties. Prior negotiations, representations, understandings NOT binding.
