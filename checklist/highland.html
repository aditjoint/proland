Section Particulars Queries to Ask the Hyperscaler / Developer
1. Land Title, Tenure & Legal Readiness Ownership Type

• Is the land freehold, leasehold, or government-allotted?

• If leasehold, what is the lease duration and renewal terms?
Title & Clearances

• Is the land title clear, dispute-free, and encumbrance-free?

• Can you share title deeds, mutation certificates, and possession proof?

• Are there any litigations, liens, or rights-of-way issues?
Conversion & Zoning

• Has the land been converted to industrial or data center use?

• Is it located within a designated Data Center Park or IT/ITES zone?

• Are construction permissions and building plan approvals in place?
Regulatory Approvals

• What local/state authorities have approved the land for DC usage?

• Any environmental, telecom, or power NOCs already obtained?
2. Location & Geophysical Suitability Seismic & Flood Zone

• What is the seismic zone rating (as per IS 1893)?

• Has a geotechnical or soil load-bearing test been performed?

• Is the land located in a flood-prone, cyclone, or low-lying area?
Elevation & Drainage

• What is the elevation above MSL (Mean Sea Level)?

• Are there proper drainage channels and flood mitigation systems?
Soil Quality

• What is the soil type and bearing capacity?

• Any soil contamination or landfill history?
Climatic Conditions

• What are the average temperature, humidity, and rainfall data?

• Any micro-climate impacts on cooling efficiency?
3. Power Availability & Electrical Infrastructure Power Source

• What is the nearest substation capacity and distance?

• Is dual-feed power available from independent substations?

• What is the grid voltage level (33kV/132kV/220kV) at the site boundary?
Load Sanction

• Has the land been allocated sanctioned power load (in MW)?

• Can capacity scale up with future phases (Phase I to N)?
Right of Way (RoW) • Are there RoW approvals for power cabling and utilities from substation to site?
Renewable Integration • Is the site eligible for renewable energy wheeling (solar/wind PPA)?
4. Connectivity & Network Access Fiber & Telco Access

• Distance to nearest optical fiber PoP / IX node?

• Availability of multiple telco providers (diverse fiber paths)?

• Is dark fiber or carrier-neutral access possible at the site?
Backhaul Redundancy

• Are there two or more physically separated fiber routes to the site?

• Who are the current network providers available in that region?
Latency Metrics • Average latency to major metro hubs (Mumbai, Chennai, Delhi)?
Edge Connectivity • Are there edge or CDN nodes nearby to minimize round-trip latency?
5. Water Supply & Cooling Support Feasibility Water Source • What are the available water sources (municipal, borewell, recycled)?
Water Rights & Permissions • Are water extraction and reuse permissions in place?
Wastewater Management • Is there a drainage/sewage system or STP requirement on-site?
Cooling Readiness • Is the water supply capacity sufficient for chilled-water or hybrid cooling systems?
6. Accessibility, Logistics & Infrastructure Transportation Access

• What is the distance to nearest highway, airport, and railway?

• Are heavy-load vehicles (transformers, generators) accessible via all-weather roads?
Proximity to Urban Centers

• How far is the nearest city or logistics hub?

• Is there access to skilled workforce nearby?
Emergency Services

• Proximity to fire station, hospitals, and police station?

• Are there fire hydrant or water bodies within response reach?
7. Site Size, Shape & Expandability Land Size • What is the total plot area (acres or hectares)?
Usable vs Non-Usable Area • How much is usable for DC construction vs setbacks, utilities, and green zones?
Shape & Layout

• Is the plot shape rectangular/square for efficient rack hall design?

• Are there topographical constraints (slopes, elevation variation)?
Future Expansion

• Is there contiguous land available for expansion?

• Are utility corridors and access roads pre-planned for Phase II / III?
8. ESG & Sustainability Considerations Green Power Feasibility • Can the site integrate onsite or nearby renewable power (solar/wind farm)?
Water & Waste Recycling • Is there a greywater reuse or rainwater harvesting provision?
Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) • Has an EIA study been completed and approved by authorities?
Noise & Air Compliance • Are local environmental noise and emission norms being met?
9. Regulatory & Incentive-Linked Queries Industrial Zone Compliance

• Does the land fall under industrial or IT/ITES DC zoning?

• Are state data center policies applicable (e.g., Maharashtra, UP, TN, Telangana)?
Incentives & Rebates • Are there tax breaks, stamp duty waivers, or power tariff rebates for DC usage?
Single-Window Clearance • Is there a dedicated DC facilitation cell (state IT department / MIDC / SIPCOT etc.)?
Building Code Compliance • Is the DC design compliant with NBC, BIS, and fire norms?
10. Validation & Closure Queries (Pre-Land Freeze) Technical Validation • Has load flow and geotechnical study been completed?
Environmental Clearance • Status of EIA and CRZ (if coastal) clearance?
Air and Noise Pollution Consent to Establish • Air and noise pollution consent to establish?
Utility Readiness • Confirm power, water, and telecom route drawings.
Documentation • Request land ownership, zoning, and pre-approval document pack.
Site Visit & Survey • When can the joint technical inspection be scheduled?
11. Summary: Key Land Selection Evaluation Parameters Land Legal Readiness Ownership, zoning, approvals, title
Geophysical Suitability Soil, seismic, flood, elevation
Power Proximity Dual feed, grid distance, MW scalability
Connectivity Fiber access, latency, redundancy
Utilities Water, cooling, drainage, access
Expansion Potential Contiguous area, phase planning
ESG Readiness Renewable access, EIA, green infra
Regulatory Advantage Incentives, clearances, compliance
